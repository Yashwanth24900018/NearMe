## AIM
To develop a website to display details about the places around my house.

## DESIGN STEPS

### STEP 1
Create a Django admin interface.

### STEP 2
Download your city map from Google.

### STEP 3
Using ```<map>``` tag name the map.

### STEP 4
Create clickable regions in the image using ```<area>``` tag.

### STEP 5
Write HTML programs for all the regions identified.

### STEP 6
Execute the programs and publish them.

Nestled at the foothills of the Alagar Hills, the temple is surrounded by lush greenery and ancient stone carvings. It is especially famous during the Chithirai Festival, when the deity is taken to the Vaigai River for a grand celebration attended by thousands of devotees.

<p>
Vandiyur Mariamman Teppakulam is a large temple tank located near the Vandiyur Mariamman Temple in Madurai. It is famous for the annual Float Festival (Teppam), where the idols of Meenakshi and Sundareswarar are placed on a float and taken around the tank. The tank is surrounded by stone steps and has a beautiful mandapam in the center. During the monsoon, it fills with water and becomes a stunning sight.
